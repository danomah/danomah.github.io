About

An IAAP certified professional in accessibility, I have over 14 years experience in the field of accessible technology, including leading a range of diverse projects.

I'm driven to create opportunities for success by creating contexts and experiences that address the needs of people and their environments, and have been fortunate to have had the opportunity to work directly with and for disabled people.

During that time I have come to truly understand and appreciate the diversity of the human condition and the market benefits of designing for all users. I work across a range of teams to ensure that accessibility is built into products, design systems and documentation.
