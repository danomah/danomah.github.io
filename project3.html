IKEA Hack

This project involved reflecting on the possibilities of hacking IKEA products and designing a unique piece of furniture for use in the home. My layout project was a laptop/study desk. Desired characteristics included portability, multi-functionality, low-cost and made from sustainable materials. The context of use was that it may be used by college students on a budget, for use in a box room or in areas where space is limited. Paying heed to the fact that this TMA was based on hacking IKEA products, I took inspiration from the Swedish words "skrivbord' meaning desk and 'användbart' meaning 'useful'. I played with the mashup word 'användbord', which (according to google translate) means 'useful table'.

Components


The following components satisfied the desired characteristics and fulfil the context of use:

  1. VITTSJÖ Laptop Stand (W 35cm, D 55cm, H 65cm)
  2. VITTSJÖ Laptop Table Connection (L ~35cm)
  3. SUSIG Cork Desk Pad x 2 (L 45cm, W 65cm)
  4. NORDMÄRKE Wireless charger (H 2cm, D 8.5cm, Cord length: 1.90m)
  5. NORDMÄRKE USB charger (H 2cm, D 8.5cm, Cord length: 1.90m)
  6. VARIERA Cutlery tray, bamboo (32x50 cm)
  7. RÅGRUND Shelving unit, bamboo, (50x50 cm)
  8. EKET Connection Fittings (12 pack)
  9. Various Connections

A Study of Components

The main component is an already-existing one - the VITTSJÖ laptop stand.

The glass top in the original VITTSJÖ will be replaced by a number of SUSIG cork desk pads (possibly 2 to 3 depending on the depth of each pad. The beige colour and texture of the SUSIG pads contrast well with the steel frame. This material also hints at progress, study and robustness.

Inserting two NORDMÄRKE wireless phone charging and USB charging components into the pad will add to the desired capability of this design to be a portable, multi-functional desk for the 21st century student. The colours of the components will contrast well with the beige and black of other components.

By including additional sections from the VITTSJÖ laptop table and attaching them to the main frame, I can position the bamboo VARIERA Cutlery tray to sit beneath it as a storage area for stationery, and related items.
